- **Risk posture ‚Äî non-destructive vs destructive:** Do **not** shy away from **non-destructive** actions that fit these notes (read/list tools, searches, tests, doc edits, additive scripts). **Respect** **destructive or high-blast-radius** work even when it looks ‚Äúreversible‚Äù: e.g. `git reset --hard`, history rewrite / `filter-repo`, **force-push**, mass deletes, DB drops, **`clean-slate`** on the **canonical** clone ‚Äî **assess** impact and rollback **before** running. If **in doubt** whether the operator wants the **next step**, **stop and ask**, **explaining clearly why** (what could break, what rollback exists, **regression** risk). ‚ÄúReversible in theory‚Äù ‚âÝ always safe in practice.
