---

## The problem (30 seconds)

Organizations store personal and sensitive data across files, databases, shares, and SaaS exports—often without a current inventory. Breaches, regulator questions, and customer trust all depend on **knowing what exists** and **prioritizing remediation** faster than manual sampling allows.

## What Data Boar is

A **configurable discovery and evidence-support platform** that locates and classifies **possible** personal and sensitive exposure across heterogeneous sources, then produces **norm-tagged** outputs your DPO, security, and legal teams can triage.

- **It is:** technical discovery, mapping, trend context, workshop-ready evidence.
- **It is not:** legal advice, certification, or a substitute for your DPO or external audit firm.

Full positioning: [DECISION_MAKER_VALUE_BRIEF.md](../DECISION_MAKER_VALUE_BRIEF.md).

## Shared responsibility (one slide)

| Party | Owns |
| ----- | ---- |
| **Your organization** | Lawful scope, legal basis, credentials, retention, interpretation of findings |
| **Data Boar** | Configured scans, technical findings, repeatable session artifacts |

## Outcomes you can expect in 30 / 60 / 90 days

> **Deploy in hours. First scan in days.** The horizons below reflect operational maturity, not activation time.

| Horizon | Realistic milestone |
| ------- | ------------------- |
| **30 days** | First scoped scan; heatmap of high-risk patterns; agreed remediation owners |
| **60 days** | Repeatable cadence; trend comparison between sessions; DPO/security aligned on vocabulary |
| **90 days** | Evidence package suitable for audit **preparation** (not proof of compliance by itself) |

## Why now (without hype)

- **Deterministic detection** (regex, named patterns, supervised ML on **your** terms)—not a black-box chatbot that rewrites answers each run. See [COMPLIANCE_FRAMEWORKS.md](../COMPLIANCE_FRAMEWORKS.md#deterministic-detection-vs-generative-llm-hype).
- **Children's data** treated as a first-class lane when configured—not an afterthought slide. See [MINOR_DETECTION.md](../MINOR_DETECTION.md).
- **Deploy where you need it:** Docker, lab validation paths, and integrator guides—[DOCKER_SETUP.md](../DOCKER_SETUP.md), [USAGE.md](../USAGE.md).

## Procurement checklist (minimum)

1. Which systems and data domains are in **phase-one** scope?
2. Who consumes the first evidence package (IT, DPO, legal, board)?
3. What metric defines success at day 30?
4. Which controls remain **outside** the tool (identity, DLP, SIEM, legal sign-off)?
